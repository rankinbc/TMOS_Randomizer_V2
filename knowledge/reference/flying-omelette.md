## Characters (12 Playable)

### Hero
- **Description**: The descendant of Isufa and the hero of the game. Once tried to fight Sabaron, but the evil magician erased his memory and sent him spinning through time.
- **Magics**: None (uses items)

### Coronya (Time Spirit)
- **Description**: Cat-like being who guides you. Uses a Rod that increases with your Rod level.
- **Magics**: Defenee, Mymy, Gygatorn

### Faruk
- **Description**: Powerful genie who helped Isufa defeat Goragora. Can attack twice per turn.
- **Magics**: Gilzade, Gygatorn

### Kebabu
- **Description**: Valkyrie/Harpy who helps access Aqua Palace. Comes with Ring and Mirror Shield.
- **Magics**: Bolttor, Seal

### Gun Meca
- **Description**: Robot with mortar board. Translates Peke Peke language. Can transform into mirror.
- **Magics**: Bolttor

### Supica
- **Description**: Flying monkey, only one who can guide through Maze Desert.
- **Magics**: Seal

### Epin
- **Description**: Guardian of Sudari, over 700 years old. Carries whistle to reveal Curly.
- **Magics**: Defenee, Tornador

### Pukin
- **Description**: Living doll from Cimaron Tree fruit. Jack-O-Lantern head.
- **Magics**: Velver

### Mustafa
- **Description**: Old man on floating crystal ball. Reveals Troll, slows enemies.
- **Magics**: Bolttor2

### Gubibi
- **Description**: Bottle with eyeball and limbs. Protects from lava.
- **Magics**: Defenee, Resealo

### Rainy
- **Description**: Rain Shrimp who drums to make rain. Can reveal Salamander.
- **Magics**: Perius, Matato

### Hassan
- **Description**: Genie and servant of Isufa. Final ally, Chapter 5.
- **Magics**: Flamol3, Caraba

### Trooper
- **Description**: Armored bulldogs. Up to 99 can be hired, 4 fight at once.
- **Magics**: None
